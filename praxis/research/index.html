FEATURED RESEARCH

No single event type predicts violence. Co-occurrence does.

Analysis of 100,184 ACLED conflict events across 13 Sahel countries at weekly district level resolution. When individual event types occur in isolation, next week attack rates drop to or below baseline. When two or more types co-occur in the same district week, lethal violence follows within 7 days.

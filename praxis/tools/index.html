How evaluators use these tools

Writing a TOR

Use the Design Advisor to justify your methodology choice. Run the Sample Size Calculator to include a defensible sample size in the TOR — not just “approximately 400 households.”

Designing a SMART Survey

Calculate exact sample size for GAM/SAM with the right DEFF. Select between 30×30 cluster design or adapted EPI method based on your context.

Analysing endline data

Upload your .xlsx straight from KoBoToolbox. Get instant frequency tables, Likert scale breakdowns, cross-tabulations, and export a formatted Word report.

Choosing an evaluation approach

Input your context — budget, timeline, comparison group feasibility, programme complexity — and get a ranked list of designs with trade-offs.

Reviewing a consultant’s proposal

Cross-check their proposed sample size against your own calculation. Verify the evaluation design fits the stated purpose. Catch methodological gaps early.

Planning in FCV settings

The Design Advisor specifically accounts for fragile and conflict-affected contexts — downranking designs that require stable access.

Training junior M&E staff

Every parameter has guided presets with contextual explanations. Junior staff can produce a defensible sample size calculation without understanding the underlying statistics.

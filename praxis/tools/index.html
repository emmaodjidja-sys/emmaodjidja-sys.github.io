Built for real work

How evaluators use these tools

📋

Writing a TOR

Use the Design Advisor to justify your methodology choice. Run the Sample Size Calculator to include a defensible sample size in the TOR — not just “approximately 400 households.”

📈

Designing a SMART Survey

Calculate exact sample size for GAM/SAM with the right DEFF. Select between 30×30 cluster design or adapted EPI method based on your context and precision needs.

📊

Analysing endline data

Upload your .xlsx straight from KoBoToolbox. Get instant frequency tables, Likert scale breakdowns, cross-tabulations by disaggregation, and export a formatted Word report for your annexes.

⚖

Choosing an evaluation approach

Input your context — budget, timeline, comparison group feasibility, programme complexity — and get a ranked list of designs with trade-offs. Not just “do an RCT.”

📊

Reviewing a consultant’s proposal

Cross-check their proposed sample size against your own calculation. Verify the evaluation design fits the stated purpose. Catch methodological gaps before they become problems.

🌎

Planning in FCV settings

The Design Advisor specifically accounts for fragile and conflict-affected contexts — downranking designs that require stable access and upranking those adapted for insecurity.

🎓

Training junior M&E staff

Every parameter has guided presets with contextual explanations. Junior staff can produce a defensible sample size calculation without understanding the underlying statistics.

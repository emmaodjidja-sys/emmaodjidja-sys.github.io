How the Early Warning Pipeline Works

1. Data Ingestion

Automated ingestion from the ACLED API. We process event streams across 13 countries at a weekly, district-level resolution.

2. Econometric Modelling

The composite model runs epidemiological surveillance logic against the data, identifying specific multi-type co-occurrence pathways such as KAFD + IED.

3. Structured Alerts

When escalation probability hits critical thresholds (up to 8.8x), the platform generates actionable alerts with a validated 7-day latency window.

Global scope, grounded in local reality.

The Sahel serves as the rigorous proof of concept for the TREMOR platform. However, the econometric model is designed for global scalability. Wherever ACLED operates, PRAXIS can deploy its predictive surveillance.

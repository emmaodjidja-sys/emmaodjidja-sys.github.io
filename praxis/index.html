// WHY THIS EXISTS

The practitioner-sovereign alternative

Every other AI for Good initiative runs through institutions. ITU through the UN system. DataKind through corporate volunteers. J-PAL through MIT. The power sits with the institution.

PRAXIS inverts this. Client-side processing. MIT license. No accounts. Offline-capable. No data transmission. The practitioner owns the methodology, the tools, and the output.

That is not a feature set. That is a political position about who owns rigorous methodology in the development sector.
